Jeff Calvert is an ultrarunner and retired soldier who writes about trail running, distance, war, and what he calls wonderment — the practice of paying close attention to the world. He lives in State College, Pennsylvania.

His writing appears at [The Rush of it All](https://rushofitall.substack.com).
